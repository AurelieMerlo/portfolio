About

I was Natural Language Processing engineer for 6 years. I worked on NLU system that extracts entities from text as temporal expressions (date, hour, duration, period, set), location (train station, aeroport, address, district…), personality, organization...by implementing linguistics resources (grammars and lexicon).

In 2017, I decided to start the Fullstack Program of LeWagon. In two months, I learned Ruby, Ruby On Rails, javascript, jQuery, Ajax, Bootstrap and deploy many projects on Heroku.

I am currently continuing my learning with personal projects and the FreeCodeCamp challenges
